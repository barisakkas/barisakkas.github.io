Account & Data Deletion Instructions

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from the Odds Tracker app.

  1. Open the Odds Tracker app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to the Danger Zone
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Enter your password to confirm

Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Alternatively, email oddstracker.dev@gmail.com to request data deletion.
